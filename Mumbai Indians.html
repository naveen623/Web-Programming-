Rohit Sharma:
1.Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket
2.He is Captian of Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League as a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off break bowler.
3. He is the vice-captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats.
Yuvraj Singh
1.Yuvraj Singh (born 12 December 1981) is a former Indian cricketer who played internationally in all forms of the game. An all-rounder who bats left-handed in the middle order.
2.Yuvraj was a member of the Indian cricket team in One Day Internationals (ODIs) between 2000 and 2017 and played his first Test match in October 2003. He was the vice-captain of the Indian ODI team between 2007 and 2008
Kieron Pollard
1.Kieron Adrian Pollard (born 12 May 1987) is a Trinidadian cricketer who captains the West Indies cricket team in limited overs cricket. An aggressive all-rounder, Pollard is a medium-pace bowler and big-hitting batsman in the middle-order.
2.In September 2019, Kieron Pollard was named as the captain of the West Indies' One Day International (ODI) and Twenty20 International (T20I) teams
